Remember

Android

Notes, lists, and reminders for Android, built around persistent notifications that stay visible until they are truly done.

Why It Exists

You've probably swiped away a task notification by mistake and then lost track of it. Remember protects your reminders from accidental dismissal — the notification resurfaces immediately, and stays visible until you say it's done.

Google Play GitHub Privacy Terms

Reminders Until Done

Mark it done and it's gone for good. Until then, Remember brings it back — quietly, not nagging — just a signal that something's still waiting.

Recurring Reminders

Daily, weekly, monthly, yearly, or patterns like "last Friday of every month." Set an end date or repeat count, and the next one schedules itself when you mark the current one done.

Notes With Context

Format notes with headings, rich text and links, nested lists, photos and attachments. A reminder about a meeting can carry the full agenda; a grocery run can have the whole list.

Notification Actions

Add a contextual action button to any reminder notification — "Call Mom" or "Get Directions" right there without opening the app.
